The Ecosystem

18 modules.
One platform.

Every component designed to eliminate manual work and protect deliverability at scale.

Backend Services
01

Automated Provisioning

Client subscribes → auto-creates Smartlead account, Zapmail workspace, 5 domains, 10 inboxes, warmup started.

02

Lead Sync Engine

Browse STFLeadlist, filter, select, push to campaigns. 2,000 leads/month cap enforced. No CSV exports.

03

Campaign Orchestration

Create sequences, assign inboxes for rotation, attach leads, set schedules, launch. Full lifecycle management.

04

Reply Handler + CRM Sync

Reply → GHL contact created/updated with tags → lead removed from campaign → suppression registry.

05

Inbox Health Monitor

Continuous polling. Breach thresholds → auto-remove from campaigns, return to warmup, notify client.

06

Billing Entitlement

Downgrade/cancel → pause campaigns, disable sending. CRM stays active. Re-enable restores everything.

07

Suppression Registry

Central safety layer. Replied/bounced/unsubscribed leads blocked from all future campaigns automatically.

Client Portal
08

Dashboard

09

Campaign Manager

4-step wizard: Info → Sequence Builder → Select Leads → Schedule & Review. Per-lead status tracking.

10

Lead Browser

STFLeadlist integration with category cards, location/date search, multi-select, push-to-campaign.

11

Inbox Manager

Health %, warmup progress, daily limits, DNS status. 3-step "Add Mailbox" wizard via Zapmail.

12

Analytics

Time range filters, stat cards, weekly chart, top campaigns by reply rate, deliverability health panel.

13

14

Settings
